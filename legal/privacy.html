Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required:

  • Email address — for product delivery and tool requests
  • SKU and purchase record — for license key generation
  • Transaction reference — from payment processor

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • No passwords
  • No payment card details
  • No browsing data or tracking cookies
  • No personal identification beyond email

3. How We Use Your Email

Your email is used exclusively to deliver your purchased files and license key, or to send the free tool pack you requested. We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

4. Data Storage

Purchase records, email addresses, and license keys are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the operator. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

5. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Binance Pay and Cryptomus. We do not handle or store any cryptocurrency wallet details or payment credentials. Refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle your payment data.

6. Data Retention

Purchase records are retained indefinitely to support license key verification and redelivery requests. Email capture records for free tool requests are retained for 12 months.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email and purchase record at any time by replying to your delivery email. Note that deletion of purchase records will invalidate your license key.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be directed by replying to any email received from Veltrix Server Tools.
